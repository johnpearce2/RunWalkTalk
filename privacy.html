Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Run Walk Talk is a run/walk interval app for iPhone and Apple Watch. This policy explains what information the app uses and why.

Information The App Uses

Run Walk Talk may use the following information when you choose to enable the related features:

  • Health and fitness data: used to save completed workouts to Apple Health when you grant permission.
  • Location data: used for route, distance, and pace features when you choose to use route tracking.
  • Nearby device information: used locally to help nearby phones and watches join the same run/walk session.

How Information Is Used

Health data is used only for workout saving. Location data is used only for route, distance, and pace features. Nearby sync information is used to start a group session and keep interval timing aligned.

Tracking And Advertising

Run Walk Talk does not use your data for advertising tracking and does not sell your personal information.

Apple Health

Health data is handled through Apple HealthKit. You control Health permissions in the Health app and iOS Settings. Run Walk Talk does not share Health data with advertisers or data brokers.

Location

Location permission is used for route tracking, distance, and pace. You can turn location access off in iOS Settings. Route features may not work without location permission.

Data Storage

Run Walk Talk is designed around on-device use and local session setup. The app does not require a user account.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact: runwalktalk@thebasepost.co.uk
